/************************************************************************
**
** Basic Folder Layout
**
** The BasicLayout definition specifies positions and sizes for common
** folder components.
**
**	HelpRect (Rect)
**
**		The space allocated to display the rollover help
**
**	BackPos (Point)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the "back" item.
**
**	ContinuePos (Point)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the "continue" item.
**
**	MainPos (Point)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the "main menu" item.
**
**	ShadeColor	(Vector)
**
**		The color to use to fill above and below the main folder area.
**		<r, g, b>
**
**	BarColor	(Vector)
**
**		The color of the bars separating the shaded area from the folder area.
**		<r, g, b>
**
**	BarHeight	(Int)
**
**		The height of the bars separating the shaded area from the folder area.
**
**	TopShadeHeight	(Int)
**
**		The height of the top shaded area (relative a 640x480 screen)
**
**	BottomShadeHeight	(Int)
**
**		The height of the bottom shaded area (relative a 640x480 screen)
**
**	LoadStringPos (Point)
**
**		The top-center position of the name of the world displayed on
**		the loading screen.
**
**	LoadPhotoPos (Point)
**
**		The top-left corner of the photo of the world displayed on
**		the loading screen.
**
**	BossRect (Rect)
**
**		The space allocated to display the boss meter
**
**
*/

/************************************************************************
**
** Selection Folder Layout
**
** The SelectionLayout definition specifies positions and sizes for
** components common to selection folders
**
**	SlotOffset (Point)
**
**		How far the slots are offset from the main list.
**
**	ListRect (Rect)
**
**		The space allocated to display the list of selectable items.
**
**	ListFontSize (Int)
**
**		Specifices which font to use for the list of selectable items.
**		0 = small font, 1 = large font
**
**	NameRect (Rect)
**
**		The space allocated to display the name of the current item.
**
**	NameFontSize (Int)
**
**		Specifices which font to use for the name of the current item.
**		0 = small font, 1 = large font
**
**	PhotoPos (String)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the picture of the current item.
**
**	DescriptionRect
**
**		The space allocated to display the description of the current item.
**
**	DescFontSize
**
**		Specifices which font to use for the description of the current item.
**		0 = small font, 1 = large font
**
**	UpArrowOffset (Point)
**
**		Offset of the upper-left corner of the up arrow from the the upper-left
**		corner of the list items.
**
**	DownArrowOffset (Point)
**
**		Offset of the upper-left corner of the down arrow from the the lower-left
**		corner of the list items.
**
*/

/************************************************************************
**
** Folder Layout
**
** The Folder Layout definitions specifies positions and sizes for folder
** components. The GenericFolder section defines default values which will
** be used if the specific folder doesn't override them.
**
** Individual folders may also have custom values specified in their section
** that are used in addition to these common definitions:
**
**	TitlePos (Point)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the title string.
**
**	TitleAlign (Integer)
**
**		The alignment of the title string:
**			0 = Left, 1 = Center, 2 = Right
**
**	PageRect (Rect)
**
**		The rectangle used by the folder items.
**		(left,top,right,bottom)
**
**	ItemSpace (Integer)
**
**		The vertical space between menu items.
**
**	ItemAlign (Integer)
**
**		The alignment of the title string:
**			0 = Left, 1 = Center, 2 = Right
**
**	FontSize
**
**		Specifices which font to use by default for items.
**		0 = small font, 1 = large font
**
**	MusicIntensity
**
**		Specifies what the menu intensity should be for this
**		folder (1-7, with 1=silence, 7=high action)
**
**	UpArrowPos (Point)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the up arrow.
**
**	DownArrowPos (Point)
**
**		The upper-left corner of the down arrow.
**
**
*/
